Privacy Policy – Dotra App

Last updated: May 2025

Thank you for using Dotra. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII). However, our app integrates third-party services, such as Google AdMob, which may collect non-personal data automatically.

2. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, usage data, and approximate location data to serve relevant ads. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for more information:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Account Data

When you register or log in, we store your email and a hashed password securely in our database. This information is used only for authentication and basic account features.

4. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. To do so, visit:
Delete My Account

5. Contact

For questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us via our official website:
https://dotraapp.com

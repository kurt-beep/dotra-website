1. Introduction

DOTRA is a digital mining project focused on fairness, simplicity, and community empowerment. No hardware investment is required. Mining is free and based solely on user engagement.

2. Mining Mechanism

Users can mine DOTRA by logging into the app daily and tapping the "Claim" button. Mining happens every 24 hours, creating a Proof-of-Engagement mechanism. Rewards are distributed based on active participation.

3. Referral System

Invite others to join DOTRA and receive an additional bonus of 0.05 DOTRA per active invited user per day. The inviter benefits; the invited user does not get a bonus themselves.

4. KYC Verification

KYC (Know Your Customer) will become mandatory for users who wish to use or transfer their mined DOTRA. Mining remains available without KYC, but unverified users will not be able to claim full control over their mined tokens.

5. Community Voting

Users with at least 10 DOTRA will be able to participate in community decisions. Votes will determine the future of the project, including technical improvements, partnerships, and potential new features.

6. Roadmap

Phase I: Beta Release of the app.
Phase II: Community growth and implementation of KYC verification.
Phase III: Testnet preparations.
Phase IV: Exchange listing and tokenization (optional).

7. Technology

DOTRA is built with Flutter for mobile apps, uses Railway and Neon for backend infrastructure, and plans to expand into the Polygon blockchain for smart contract operations in the future.
